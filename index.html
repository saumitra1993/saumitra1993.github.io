I graduated from NIT Nagpur with a Computer Science degree and have worked almost exclusively at early-stage startups since then. I call myself an imaginative rationalist; basically creativity + technology usually amplifies the quality of my output.

In college, I co-founded a pioneering app for college communication that has gone on to become a part of the college culture.

I now help craft seamless shopping experiences at Swym for tens of thousands of e-commerce stores all over the world and delivering millions of dollars in attributable sales via our platform.

At Swym, I have fallen in love with functional programming and JavaScript. This fascination has led me to build systems that I myself marvel at. :)
